About

MellowViews reviews music, film and literature. We are lovers of art and feel that it should be properly interpreted if it is to have any use for people in society.

Why we do not rate artistic works

Art is not a creation like most, it's level of quality can not be measured against any one particular scale. There are many factors that determine what sort of art someone will be drawn to and the embodiment of those factors, we can refer to as taste. Now, because it is usually a fruitless endeavor to argue about taste, MellowViews will not attempt to do so.
